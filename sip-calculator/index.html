What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It helps investors build wealth over time through disciplined investing and compounding.

How SIP Calculator Works?

This SIP calculator uses monthly investment amount, expected annual return rate and investment duration to estimate the future value of your investment.
