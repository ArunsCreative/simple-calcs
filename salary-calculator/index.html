What is Salary In-Hand?

Salary in-hand is the actual amount an employee receives every month after deductions such as Provident Fund (PF), Professional Tax and ESI.

How to Use Salary Calculator?

  1. Enter your annual CTC
  2. Enter PF percentage (usually 12%)
  3. Enter professional tax
  4. Select ESI applicability
  5. Click calculate to view take-home salary
