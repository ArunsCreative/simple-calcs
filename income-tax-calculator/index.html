What is Income Tax?

Income tax is a tax levied by the Government of India on the income earned by individuals. The tax amount depends on income level and the tax regime chosen.

New vs Old Tax Regime

The new tax regime offers lower tax rates but does not allow most deductions, while the old regime allows deductions like 80C, 80D, and HRA.
